# Questions For Synthesis

- What is RitmaOS actually productizing?
- Is the first product a template, CLI, app, service, or methodology?
- Who is the first target customer?
- What should stay as raw knowledge versus become product doctrine?
- What parts of the method are unique enough to build around?
- What should be delayed until more source material is collected?
- What would an MVP workspace generator need to create?
- What can be offered as a service before software exists?
