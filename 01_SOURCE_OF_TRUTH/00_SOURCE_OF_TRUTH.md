# RitmaOS — Source of Truth

## One-line definition

RitmaOS is a filesystem-based orchestration layer for AI-powered work.

It turns business workflows into structured, AI-readable folders with identity, context, references, staged workflows, prompts, outputs, review checkpoints, memory, and improvement loops.

## Core thesis

Most businesses are using AI like a chat box. RitmaOS turns AI into an operating system for work.

The product is not the AI model. The product is the operating layer around AI.

## What RitmaOS is

RitmaOS is a structured workspace system that helps a business make AI useful, repeatable, and reliable.

It provides:

- Persistent context
- Clear task direction
- Workflow stages
- Routing rules
- Reference knowledge
- Human review checkpoints
- Output storage
- Memory and improvement loops

## What RitmaOS is not

RitmaOS is not:

- A prompt pack
- A chatbot
- A single automation
- A wrapper around one model
- A replacement for people
- A full autonomous agent platform on day one

## Key product belief

AI models are powerful but unstable on their own. To make them useful inside a real business, they need architecture around them.

That architecture includes files, stages, rules, context, outputs, review, and feedback.

## The important pattern

Folder = memory  
Prompt = direction  
Workflow = orchestration  
Review = reliability  
Memory = improvement

## The moat

The moat is not the model. The moat is the client-specific operating layer:

- Their workflows
- Their terminology
- Their clients
- Their services
- Their rules
- Their examples
- Their quality standards
- Their review process
- Their accumulated memory

## Current product direction

The best first version is likely a workspace generator, not a full SaaS app.

The product starts by asking intake questions, then generating a structured AI workspace for a specific business or workflow.

Example:

```text
ritma init web-agency
ritma init property-management
ritma init dental-clinic
ritma init ai-automation-agency
```

## Working positioning

RitmaOS turns business workflows into AI-readable folders.

RitmaOS is the reliability layer around AI.

RitmaOS is the layer above the prompt.

RitmaOS routes business context to the right AI, tool, workflow, and human checkpoint.
