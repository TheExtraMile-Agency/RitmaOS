# Workflow Lifecycle

A RitmaOS workflow can move through staged folders:

1. Intake: gather request, source, constraints, and success criteria.
2. Analysis: break down the work and identify open questions.
3. Draft: produce working outputs.
4. Review: evaluate against constraints and source material.
5. Delivery: package final outputs.
6. Memory: store decisions, references, and reusable context.

This lifecycle is a working model for research and product exploration.
