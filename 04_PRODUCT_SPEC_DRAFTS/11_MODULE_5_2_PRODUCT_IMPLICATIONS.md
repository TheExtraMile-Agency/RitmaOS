## Product Thesis Implication

Module 5.2 suggests RitmaOS should support partnership and business-structure mapping, not only system or workspace mapping.

The product opportunity is to expose how work compounds across clients, assets, templates, proof, and future offers.

## Partnership Layer Map Workflow

Draft workflow:

1. User describes their role, service, client base, or partnership.
2. RitmaOS maps the 60% business infrastructure.
3. RitmaOS maps the 30% orchestration between work streams.
4. RitmaOS identifies the 10% AI or technology capability.
5. RitmaOS runs the removal test: would the business still work if AI were swapped for another technology?

Outputs:

- business infrastructure inventory,
- orchestration map,
- AI dependency notes,
- architecture versus feature-demo diagnosis.

## Revenue Stream Mapper

RitmaOS could turn existing work into a connected revenue stream map.

Potential stream types:

- retainer,
- day rate,
- revenue share,
- licensing,
- course delivery,
- consulting,
- content.

The product should identify what infrastructure already exists, what needs to be built, and whether each stream improves the others.

## In-House Cost Comparison Template

The in-house comparison can become a reusable value-anchor artifact.

Template sections:

- client-side roles required,
- estimated salaries,
- ramp time,
- ongoing overhead,
- total annual cost,
- user's fee,
- speed to first deliverable,
- reusable assets included,
- honest cost reduction percentage,
- caveats that prevent the comparison from becoming misleading.

This supports "sell architecture, not AI" by showing the business structure and delivery advantage.

## Orchestration Loop / Flywheel Template

Draft loop artifact:

```text
work delivered -> proof generated -> data/byproduct captured -> next engagement -> reusable asset -> next buyer
```

The template should identify leaks such as:

- proof not published,
- training data not reused,
- case studies not converted,
- templates not productized,
- client follow-up not routed,
- outputs not feeding the next offer.

## Client Conversation Prep

RitmaOS could include a "Sell Architecture, Not AI" preparation template.

Potential sections:

- who the user is talking to,
- user's default pitch,
- five infrastructure/orchestration questions,
- what each answer reveals,
- how the conversation naturally leads to the user's offering,
- rewritten conversational pitch that introduces AI only after the 60% and 30% are visible.

## Filesystem Implication

The file system should expose compounding work.

Possible workspace structure:

```text
partnership-map/
revenue-streams/
in-house-comparisons/
orchestration-loops/
client-conversation-prep/
proof-assets/
case-studies/
content-byproducts/
future-offers/
```

This extends RitmaOS from organizing tasks to routing byproducts into reusable assets and future business opportunities.

## MVP Implication

1. Partnership Layer Map.

3. In-House Cost Comparison.
