# Product Spec Workflow

1. Start from raw modules and extracted frameworks.
2. Draft product implications in `04_PRODUCT_SPEC_DRAFTS/`.
3. Keep MVP, business model, and architecture claims marked as draft until decided.
4. Update `04_PRODUCT_SPEC_DRAFTS/00_SPEC_INDEX.md`.
5. Move durable decisions into `06_DECISIONS_LOG/DECISIONS.md`.
