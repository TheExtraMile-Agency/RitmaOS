# Synthesis Workflow

Synthesis turns raw modules and extracted frameworks into working product understanding.

1. Identify the source module or framework.
2. Write interpretations outside `02_RAW_MODULES/`.
3. Separate evidence from assumptions.
4. Add unresolved questions to `07_SYNTHESIS_QUEUE/QUESTIONS_FOR_SYNTHESIS.md`.
5. Record durable decisions only in `06_DECISIONS_LOG/DECISIONS.md`.
