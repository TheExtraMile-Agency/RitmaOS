# RitmaOS — Business Model

## Product ladder

### Free / low-cost

Starter templates and reference cards.

Examples:

- AI workspace starter kit
- Prompt structure reference
- Workflow folder template
- Simple business context template

Purpose: build trust and demonstrate the method.

### Paid setup

Done-for-you workspace buildout.

Offer:

“We turn one of your workflows into an AI-readable operating system.”

Possible price range:

- Basic workflow setup: $500–$1,500
- Business workspace setup: $1,500–$5,000
- Multi-workflow setup: $5,000+

### Monthly retainer

Ongoing optimization.

Includes:

- Updating context files
- Adding new workflows
- Reviewing outputs
- Improving prompts
- Cleaning memory
- Auditing quality
- Adding automations

Possible price range:

- $300–$1,500/month depending on scope

### Productized SaaS later

A software layer for generating, managing, and running RitmaOS workspaces.

## What to give away

Give away the book layer:

- Basic templates
- Simple prompt examples
- General folder structure
- Educational content

## What to charge for

Charge for the movie and video game layers:

- Workflow mapping
- Business-specific context engineering
- Custom workspace architecture
- Tool routing
- Automation flows
- Review systems
- Data/memory improvement loops

## Pricing principle

Do not price as “AI prompts.”

Price as:

- Workflow clarity
- Time saved
- Reduced rework
- Standardized team output
- Better client delivery
- Business knowledge captured into a system

## Possible first offer

### AI Workflow Operating System Setup

For small businesses using AI chaotically.

Deliverables:

- 60–90 minute intake
- One AI-readable business workspace
- One custom workflow folder
- Prompt templates
- Review checklist
- Output storage structure
- 30-minute handoff call

Positioning:

“Stop using AI like random chat. We will turn one workflow into a repeatable AI-powered process.”
